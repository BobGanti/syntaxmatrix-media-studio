But because without open-source AI, the future becomes closed by default.

And a closed-by-default AI future is not democratic.

It is not resilient.

It is not competitive.

It is not sovereign.

And it is not safe in the long term.

So yes, use OpenAI.

Use Anthropic.

Use Google.

Use the best frontier models when they make sense.

But do not stop building open alternatives.

Do not stop supporting open-source models.

Do not stop demanding auditability.

Do not stop asking who controls the model, who controls the data, who controls access, and who benefits from restriction.

Because the future of AI should not be closed AI versus unsafe AI.

That is a false choice.

The future should be responsible AI which is open.

Safe where necessary.

Auditable where possible.

Open where responsible.

Governed by humans.

And accessible enough that intelligence does not become the private property of a few powerful gatekeepers.

That is the conversation we need to have now.

Not later.

Now.
